3. STREAK SYSTEM
How It Works
Daily Activity Tracking:

User must view at least 1 ad (for earners) or get at least 1 click (for advertisers) to count

Streak increases by 1 each consecutive day

Missing a day resets streak to 0

Streak protection (discussed below)

5. LEVELS & XP SYSTEM
Experience Points (XP)
How Users Earn XP:

View 1 ad: +5 XP

Click on ad: +10 XP

Complete campaign: +50 XP

Refer user: +200 XP

Complete achievement: +100 XP

Complete daily challenge: +150 XP

Maintain 7-day streak: +500 XP

Level System:

Level 1: 0 - 100 XP (Newbie)
Level 2: 100 - 250 XP (Beginner)
Level 3: 250 - 500 XP (Learner)
Level 4: 500 - 1,000 XP (Regular)
Level 5: 1,000 - 2,000 XP (Active)
...
Level 20: 50,000+ XP (Legend)
Level Benefits:

Higher levels = Higher earning rates per ad (5% boost per 5 levels)

Exclusive badges
